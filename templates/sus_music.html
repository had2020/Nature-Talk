To scan the Spotify code, you need to:

  • Launch the Spotify app on your phone or tablet
  • Tap the Search button from the toolbar at the bottom of the app.
  • Tap the camera icon on the top right side of the screen to bring up the camera viewfinder.
  • Point your camera at the Spotify code to scan.
  • The audio or playlist should open.
